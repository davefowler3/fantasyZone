About Us

Fantasy Zone Comics opened in 1988, making us the longest running comic book store in RI. We are a husband and wife team who love comics and read every title we carry.

Our inventory includes over 60,000 back issues and a full line of new titles from publishers big and small. We also have a large selection of used paperbacks and graphic novels. If you like to read, or just want to escape reality for a bit, there is something waiting for you within these walls.
